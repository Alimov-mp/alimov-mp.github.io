CITIES OF TJK

There are 18 cities in Tajikistan. One of them, the country’s capital Dushanbe city, has the status of a separate administrative-territorial unit of the first order, three cities have the status of a city of republican subordination, 7 cities have the status of a city of regional subordination, and 6 are the cities of regional subordination. Most of the cities are located in the northern part of the country on the Sogd region.


  • Gafurov - the city since 1978. Now the village.
  • Navabad - the city in the years 1950-1959. Now the village.
  • Panj - the city since 1953. Now the village.
  • Shurab - the city in the years 1952-2007. Now the village.
